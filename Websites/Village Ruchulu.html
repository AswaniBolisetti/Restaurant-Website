From our menu
Chicken Dum Biriyani

Succulent pieces of marinated chicken, fragrant basmati rice, and aromatic spices cooked together in perfect harmony, creating a tantalizing medley of flavors and textures.

Fry Piece Chicken Biriyani

Crispy, golden-fried chicken pieces served atop a bed of flavorful biryani rice, offering a delightful blend of crunch and spice in every bite.

Chicken Tandoori

Juicy chicken pieces marinated in a blend of yogurt and spices, roasted to perfection in a tandoor, resulting in smoky, charred, and incredibly tender meat that's bursting with flavor.

Veg Meals

A wholesome vegetarian feast featuring a colorful array of fresh vegetables, lentils, and aromatic spices, served with rice or bread, delivering a symphony of flavors and nutrients in every bite.

Fish Curry

A rich and spicy fish curry made with fresh catches of the day, simmered in a luscious coconut or tomato-based sauce, creating a deliciously tangy and satisfying seafood delight.

Prawns Curry

Plump, succulent prawns cooked in a flavorful curry sauce, with a perfect balance of spices and coconut, providing a mouthwatering seafood experience.

Sambar Rice

A South Indian classic that combines steaming hot rice with a hearty lentil-based vegetable stew, offering a harmonious blend of flavors, textures, and spices.

Paneer Tikka

Cubes of paneer (Indian cheese) marinated in a spicy yogurt mixture, skewered and grilled to perfection, resulting in a charred, smoky, and irresistibly creamy appetizer.

Garlic Naan

Soft and fluffy Indian bread infused with aromatic garlic and fresh herbs, perfect for scooping up delectable curries and sauces, making every bite a fragrant and savory delight.

About Us

At Village Ruchulu, we transport you to the heart of India through our authentic Indian cuisine. Our commitment to quality begins in the serene villages of India, where we source the freshest ingredients. What sets us apart is our use of spices directly imported from the source, infusing every dish with true Indian flavors. Our skilled chefs craft a diverse menu, from North Indian tandoori delights to South Indian aromatic curries, providing a culinary journey for your taste buds. We believe in offering not just a meal but an unforgettable dining experience, marked by our warm atmosphere, attentive service, and the ability to customize your meal to your preferences. Join us at Village Ruchulu to savor the essence of India with every bite, and let us take you on a gastronomic journey like no other.
